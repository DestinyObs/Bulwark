About Us

Who are we?

Bulwark Development Initiatives (BDI) is a non-governmental development organization established to make significant impact in Nigeria. Founded with the aim of fostering sustainable development and improving the lives of communities across the nation, BDI has become a beacon of hope and catalyst for positive change. This narrative highlights BDI's key initiatives and achievements, showcasing her commitment to empowerment and progress.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Bulwark Development Initiatives works for the social development and integration of underprivileged individuals, groups and communities in Nigeria. The focus is on the marginalized groups, including children, young adults, and the elderly as they often face significanct barriers to accessing education , employment and other opportunities that can help them reach their full potentials . Investing in the youth and other underprivileged groups is crucial for building a strong and prosperous economy . By providing these groups with the necessary resources and support, such as education and skills training Bulwark Development Initiatives can help them break the cycle of poverty and contribute to the economic growth and development in Nigeria

Our work aims to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social isolation and to restore hope for a better future. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society.

Vision

Specific Objectives

The Organization shall have the following specific objects:

To work for the social development of underprivileged individuals, groups, and communities in Nigeria, especially girls and marginalized groups.

To work for the social development of underprivileged individuals, groups, and communities in Nigeria, especially girls and marginalized groups.

To encourage healthy promotion for School age children in school and out of school through community led total hygiene and sanitation education and communication change behavior strategies

To encourage healthy promotion for School age children in school and to assist in the process of social integration and personal realisation of underprivileged children, young people, adults and familiesd out of school through community led total hygiene and sanitation education and communication change behavior strategies

To assist in the process of social integration and personal realisation of underprivileged children, young people, adults and families

To endorse the human rights and in particular the rights of the children and young people as well as the rights of underprivileged groups and communities

To encourage and popularize voluntary service and promotion of financial inclusion for entrepreneur capacity Development

The projects of Bulwark Development Initiatives range from specific one off humanitarian actions in response to a specific urgent need to long- term capacity development and delivery of basic education and development services in marginalized communities.
It aims to help the most vulnerable members of our society and include work on different levels such as

  • Analysis and assessment of needs
  • Developing programmes and strategies for development
  • Empowering and developing local communities
  • Conduct action research and impact evaluation to provide feedback to Education ,health and social development stakeholders in Nigeria and other countries
  • Interaction with stakeholders to participate in development of inclusive reforms that Will help Nigeria to achieve her Development agenda
  • Provision of inclusive Education and gender and safeguarding training for School managers and Education Policy makers
